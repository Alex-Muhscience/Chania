Frequently Asked Questions

You can apply for our programs by visiting the Programs page and clicking on the "Apply Now" button for your chosen program. You'll need to fill out an application form and submit required documents. Our admissions team will review your application and get back to you within 3-5 business days.

We offer both free and paid programs. Our beginner-level programs are often free or heavily subsidized, while advanced and specialized programs may have tuition fees. We also offer scholarships and payment plans for those who qualify.

Requirements vary by program. Most beginner programs require basic computer literacy and a high school diploma. Intermediate and advanced programs may require prior knowledge in the field. All programs require commitment to complete the coursework and participate actively.

Yes, we have a dedicated career services team that works with our network of employer partners to help graduates find employment opportunities. We offer career counseling, resume reviews, interview preparation, and job matching services.

Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with organizations, companies, and individuals who share our mission. Partnership opportunities include program sponsorship, hiring our graduates, providing internships, volunteering as mentors, and more. Please contact our partnerships team for more information.

There are several ways to support our work: financial donations, in-kind donations (equipment, software licenses), volunteering your time and expertise, spreading awareness about our programs, or hiring our graduates. Visit our "Get Involved" page or contact us to discuss how you can help.
