About Skills for Africa

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind our initiative

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Skills for Africa began as a small initiative in Nairobi, Kenya, with the goal of addressing the growing digital skills gap among African youth. What started as a single training center has now grown into a pan-African movement with operations in 25 countries across the continent.

Our journey has been fueled by the belief that every young African deserves access to quality digital skills training and the opportunity to participate in the global digital economy.

Our Mission

To empower African youth with in-demand digital skills through innovative training programs, strategic partnerships, and ecosystem building, creating pathways to meaningful employment and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Our Vision

A future where African youth are at the forefront of the global digital economy, driving innovation and economic growth across the continent.

Our Core Values

Inclusivity

We believe in creating opportunities for all, regardless of gender, background, or location.

Innovation

We constantly seek new and better ways to deliver impact and solve challenges.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, accountability, and ethical practices.

Impact

We measure our success by the tangible difference we make in people's lives.

Our Journey

2018

Founded in Nairobi

Launched our first training center in Nairobi with 50 students and 3 instructors.

2019

Expanded to 5 Countries

Opened centers in Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, Ghana, and Rwanda.

2020

Digital Pivot

25 Countries
