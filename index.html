About Skills for Africa

Skills for Africa is a pan-African initiative dedicated to bridging the digital skills gap and empowering young Africans with the technical and soft skills needed to thrive in the digital economy.

Our mission is to create pathways to meaningful employment and entrepreneurship opportunities for African youth through innovative training programs, strategic partnerships, and ecosystem building.
